## methodology
### querying strategy
#### Query 1 (Amey)
Used 2 models (GradientBoosting + Ridge) to compute disagreement and failure similarity scores. Hand-picked biologically important positions (charged residues R, K, D, Y) in the catalytic region. Selected 100 mutations across 27 unique positions (range 40-509), with multiple mutations per position (e.g. 5 at R156, 5 at Y118, 5 at D99).

#### Query 2 (Rishi)
Built a disagreement-based active learning pipeline (`Model Attemps/disagreement_query.py`). Trained 5 models (GradientBoosting, HistGradientBoosting, Ridge, ExtraTrees, RandomForest) on combined training data (train.csv + queryGroundTruth1.csv = 1240 examples). For each test mutation, computed variance across all 5 model predictions. Added a diversity constraint: divided the 656-length protein into 20 bins (~33 positions each) and selected the top 5 highest-disagreement mutations from each bin. This ensures coverage across the full protein rather than clustering in one region. Result: 100 mutations across 74 unique positions (range 1-637), with 98/100 from positions not in training data.

## model development 
### model architecture
`MLB26_Hack.ipynb` encodes each mutant as scaled site index, 20-D mutant one-hot, BLOSUM62 score, and hydrophobicity, volume, and charge deltas (wild-type to mutant). XGBoost `XGBRegressor` fits boosted trees on this tabular input.

### training details
Random 80/20 train/validation split (seed 0); training uses the train portion only. XGBoost uses `rank:pairwise`, 300 estimators, depth 3, learning rate 0.03, row and column subsample 0.8, `random_state=42`, then predicts on the test feature matrix.

## evaluation process
### model perfomance analysis
Validation Spearman scores (80/20 split, trained on train.csv + query1 = 1240 examples):
- GradientBoosting: 0.4912
- HistGradientBoosting: 0.4848
- RandomForest: 0.4501
- ExtraTrees: 0.4113
- Ridge: 0.2727

Kaggle scores (30% hidden test set):
- XGBoost rank:pairwise (train.csv only, 1140 examples): 0.34263
- 4-model ensemble without Ridge (train.csv + query1, 1240 examples): 0.34641
